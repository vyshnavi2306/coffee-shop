About us

At Coffee Bliss, we’re more than just a coffee shop—we’re a community. Founded by coffee lovers, for coffee lovers, our goal has always been to create a warm space where every visitor feels at home. We pride ourselves on using ethically sourced beans and sustainable practices. From bean to brew, every step is done with passion. Whether you're sipping a latte, working on your laptop, or chatting with friends, our cozy space provide the perfect backdrop for any moment.
